Curry Recipe

A completed bowl of curry with rice and pickled veggies

This is a Japanese dish that first caught on with the Japanese Navy as an easily storable food that could be rehydrated rapidly. It was useful in preventing scurvy, a common malaise amongst Japanese sailors.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Stock
  • 3 Potatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 Carrots
  • 1 lb. Chicken
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Curry rue

Steps

  1. Prep by chopping the vegetables and minicng the garlic
  2. Cook the chicken in the pot with the butter and garlic
  3. Saute the vegtables in a seperate pot
  4. Once the chicken is thoroughly cooked, add in the broth and cooked veggies
  5. Add in the curry rue and stir until dissolved
  6. Allow the curry to simmer for 10 minutes
  7. Serve over a bed of rice with pickled eggies
