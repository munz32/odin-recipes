Eggs Recipe

Finished eggs garnished with chives

Everyones favorite breakfast, a simple dish of scrambled eggs. A wonderful combination of yellow protein.

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • Corn Starch
  • Butter
  • Toast
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Crach the four eggs into a bowl
  2. Mix corn starch and water into a seperate bowl
  3. Mix the corn starch and eggs
  4. Slice butter and mix into the eggs
  5. Saute eggs in a pan over high heat for 1 minute while mixing continuously
  6. Garnish eggs with chives
  7. Serve with toast
